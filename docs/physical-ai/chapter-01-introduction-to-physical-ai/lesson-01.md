# Lesson 1.1: What is Physical AI?

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the definition of Physical AI
- Recognize applications of Physical AI in daily life
- Identify the relationship between AI and physical systems

## Key Concepts
- Physical AI definition
- AI + physical computing
- Real-world applications

![Physical AI System Diagram](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1581092580497-e0d23cbdf1dc?ixlib=rb-4.0.3&ixid=M3wxMjA3fDB8MHxwaG90by1wYWdlfHx8fGVufDB8fHx8fA%3D%3D&auto=format&fit=crop&w=600&q=80)

Physical AI represents the integration of artificial intelligence algorithms with physical systems that can perceive, reason, and actuate in the real world. This integration enables systems to interact dynamically with their environment.

## Hands-On Activity: Simple Sensor System
In this activity, you will build a basic sensor system that responds to environmental changes.

### What You'll Need
- Computer with internet access
- Web browser
- Text editor

### Steps
1. Explore the concept of sensors
2. Understand how AI makes decisions
3. Connect sensor data to AI systems

## Expected Outputs & Validation Criteria
- You'll understand the concept of Physical AI
- You'll identify examples of Physical AI in daily life

## Reflection Questions
- What are some examples of Physical AI you encounter daily?
- How might AI improve physical systems in your environment?

## Follow-Up Exploration Tasks
- Research a real-world application of Physical AI
- Find an article about recent advances in Physical AI

## Glossary Items Introduced
- Physical AI: The integration of artificial intelligence with physical systems
- Sensor: A device that detects changes in the environment

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- Confusing Physical AI with traditional AI: Physical AI involves interaction with the physical world

## Safety and Ethical Considerations
- Always consider privacy and security when implementing physical AI systems

## Learning Objectives
- Understand natural language processing for embedded systems
- Learn about speech recognition fundamentals
- Implement basic text processing on resource-constrained devices

## Key Concepts
- Embedded NLP
- Speech recognition basics
- Text processing on constrained devices

Natural language processing enables Physical AI systems to understand and respond to human language. For embedded systems, this presents unique challenges due to the computational complexity of language processing. This lesson covers the fundamentals of implementing NLP techniques on resource-constrained devices.

## Hands-On Activity: Basic Text Processing
In this activity, you will implement basic NLP operations on a simulated embedded system.

### What You'll Need
- Computer with internet access
- NLP libraries for embedded systems
- Text samples

### Steps
1. Implement basic text preprocessing
2. Apply simple language models
3. Optimize for computational efficiency
4. Test on limited resources

## Expected Outputs & Validation Criteria
- Successfully implement basic NLP operations
- Optimize for computational constraints
- Understand limitations of embedded NLP

## Reflection Questions
- What are the main challenges of NLP on embedded devices?
- How can you simplify language understanding for specific domains?

## Follow-Up Exploration Tasks
- Research keyword spotting for efficient speech processing
- Explore domain-specific language models

## Glossary Items Introduced
- Natural Language Processing (NLP): AI field focused on understanding and generating human language
- Keyword Spotting: Identifying specific words or phrases in speech

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- Using full language models on embedded devices: Use simplified or domain-specific models
- Ignoring acoustic environment: Account for background noise and audio quality

## Safety and Ethical Considerations
- Protect privacy of voice and text data
- Consider bias in language processing models

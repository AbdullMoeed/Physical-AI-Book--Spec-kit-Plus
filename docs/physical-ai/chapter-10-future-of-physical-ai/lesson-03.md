# Lesson 10.3: Professional Practices and Career Paths

## Learning Objectives
- Understand professional practices in Physical AI development
- Explore career opportunities in the field
- Develop professional skills for Physical AI practitioners

## Key Concepts
- Professional development
- Career pathways
- Professional practices

![Physical AI System Diagram](/img/physical-ai-system-diagram.svg)

The field of Physical AI offers diverse career opportunities across industries including robotics, IoT, smart systems, and automation. Understanding professional practices, continuing education requirements, and career development strategies is essential for success in this rapidly evolving field.

## Hands-On Activity: Career Planning Exercise
In this activity, you will explore different career paths in Physical AI and plan professional development.

### What You'll Need
- Computer with research tools
- Career planning resources
- Professional development templates

### Steps
1. Research career opportunities in Physical AI
2. Identify required skills and qualifications
3. Plan professional development activities
4. Create a professional development roadmap

## Expected Outputs & Validation Criteria
- Clear understanding of Physical AI career opportunities
- Personalized professional development plan
- Identification of relevant professional practices

## Reflection Questions
- What career path in Physical AI aligns with your interests?
- How do you continue learning as the field evolves?

## Follow-Up Exploration Tasks
- Join professional organizations in related fields
- Attend conferences and workshops on Physical AI

## Glossary Items Introduced
- Professional Development: Continuing education and skill development
- Career Path: Progression of roles and responsibilities in a field

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- Stopping learning after formal education: Commit to lifelong learning
- Not networking professionally: Build connections in the field

## Safety and Ethical Considerations
- Maintain professional standards in all work
- Consider the ethical implications of your professional decisions

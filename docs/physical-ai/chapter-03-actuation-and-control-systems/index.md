# Chapter 3: Actuation and Control Systems

This chapter covers how AI systems can affect the physical world through actuators and control mechanisms, creating responsive and interactive systems.

## Overview

Actuators are the muscles of Physical AI systems. This chapter explores different types of actuators, how to control them, and how to design effective control systems. Understanding actuation is essential for creating Physical AI systems that can perform useful actions in the real world.

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will:
- Understand different types of actuators and their applications
- Know how to control various types of actuators
- Be able to design effective control systems
- Understand feedback mechanisms and their importance
- Learn about safety considerations in actuator design

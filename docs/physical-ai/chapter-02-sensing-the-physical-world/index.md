# Chapter 2: Sensing the Physical World

This chapter explores how physical systems can perceive their environment through various sensors, collecting data that AI systems can interpret and act upon.

## Overview

Sensors are the eyes and ears of Physical AI systems. In this chapter, we'll explore different types of sensors, how they work, and how to integrate them into AI systems. Understanding sensing is crucial for building Physical AI systems that can effectively interact with the environment.

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will:
- Understand different types of sensors and their applications
- Know how to connect and read data from various sensors
- Be able to process and interpret sensor data
- Understand the limitations and constraints of sensor systems
- Learn about sensor fusion techniques to combine multiple inputs

# Safety Guidelines

![Safety in Physical AI](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1497366754035-f200968a6e72?ixlib=rb-4.0.3&ixid=M3wxMjA3fDB8MHxwaG90by1wYWdlfHx8fGVufDB8fHx8fA%3D%3D&auto=format&fit=crop&w=2370&q=80)

This guide provides important safety information for working with physical AI systems. Safety considerations include:

- Electrical safety when working with circuits
- Mechanical safety with moving parts
- Software safety to prevent unintended actions
- Data privacy and security
- Environmental considerations

Always prioritize safety in every project and experiment.

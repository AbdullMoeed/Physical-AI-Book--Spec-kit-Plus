# Physical AI Book Introduction

This is the introduction to the Physical AI Book curriculum. This book bridges artificial intelligence and physical computing systems, transforming readers from curious beginners to capable practitioners who can create interactive, intelligent physical systems.

## What You'll Learn

- How to integrate AI with physical systems
- Working with sensors and actuators
- Building responsive environments
- Implementing machine learning on physical devices
- Creating computer vision applications for physical systems
- Connecting your systems to networks and cloud services

## Getting Started

Begin with Chapter 1 to understand the fundamentals, then progress through each chapter sequentially to build your skills and knowledge systematically.

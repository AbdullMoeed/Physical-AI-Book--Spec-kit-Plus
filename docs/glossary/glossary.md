# Glossary

![Glossary of Physical AI Terms](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1547658719-da2b51169166?ixlib=rb-4.0.3&ixid=M3wxMjA3fDB8MHxwaG90by1wYWdlfHx8fGVufDB8fHx8fA%3D%3D&auto=format&fit=crop&w=2370&q=80)

This is the comprehensive glossary of terms used throughout the Physical AI Book. Key terms include:

- Physical AI: The integration of artificial intelligence with physical systems
- Sensor: A device that detects and responds to environmental inputs
- Actuator: A component that moves or controls a mechanism
- Edge Computing: Processing data near its source rather than in the cloud
- Machine Learning: Algorithms that improve through experience

Refer to this glossary when encountering unfamiliar terminology.
